About Me

I am Durgesh Singh from Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, India. I am passionate about photography and travelling. Love experiencing new places and their culture.My favourite till date has been trip to Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand. You experience peace, joy, lush green teak trees and the roads. My passion for photography started when as a child I watched an advertisement about Incredible India. It was so eye soothing to watch that India is just not diversified culturally but also geographically. My dream is to travel worldwide to capture beautiful shots and I wish to fullfil this desire of mine someday!! :)
